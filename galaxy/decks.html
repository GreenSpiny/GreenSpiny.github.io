Pure core

Pure is the most common Galaxy variant. While it can use some off-archetypal techs, most of its plays are Photon or Galaxy reliant. It functions well even under Galaxy Trance lock.

Dragon Variant core

Though not the focus of this website, the Dragon variant is a popular build choice. It uses generic Chaos Dragon support to summon several Dragon Link and xyz monsters.
