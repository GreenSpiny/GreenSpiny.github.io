Pure core

Pure is the most common Galaxy variant and the focus of this website.
It can use some off-archetypal techs, but functions well even under Galaxy Trance lock.

Pure example deck

Techs and side cards vary by format.

Utopic Variant

Leaning into its ability to put multiple Rank 4 and 8 xyz monsters on the field, Galaxy can field extra formidable boards with Rank-Up-Magic and Utopia's ZS archetype. Number 99: Utopia Dragonrar summons multiple Number monsters from the Extra deck, and access to Number F0: Utopic Draco Future is consistent. The addition of Photon Delta Wing is a great boon to this variant.

Therion Variant

Therion monsters are popular additions to archetypes making Rank 4s or 8s. With Galaxy Soldier and Photon Orbital as targets for Therion "King" Regulus - the latter of which can perform its search after being equipped - Galaxy has innate synergy as well. This variant breaks Galaxy Trance lock more often than pure, and prioritizes Gigantic "Champion" Sargas and Springans Merrymaker routes instead.
