Matchups

Get tips for dealing with specific opponents. (work in progress)

Kashtira Arise-Heart is public enemy number one. His continuous banishing effect not only cripples your GY setup, but it disables the summon of Galaxy Soldier and search of Photon Orbital. Eliminate him at all costs.

Divine Arsenal AA-Zeus - Sky Thunder is a major threat to Kashtira boards. You can steal Arise-Heart with Triple Tactics Talent and use him to make Zeus. The targeting immunity of Number 41: Bagooska the Terribly Tired Tapir or battle protection of Photon Orbital can be helpful when manually climbing into Zeus.

Hyper Galaxy is a searchable out to Arise-Heart, as Galaxy Wizard's search still functions under banish effects. Photon Thrasher makes ideal tribute fodder, as do Photon Sanctuary tokens. Note that tokens cannot be banished face-down by Kashtira effects.

Starliege Photon Blast Dragon forces interrupts by protecting your other monsters from Kashtira effects, and threatening to summon a Galaxy-Eyes Photon Dragon from hand that can trade with Arise-Heart in battle.

Going first against Kastira is much easier than going second. The lingering targeting protection of Galaxy-Eyes Cipher X Dragon turns off nearly all their outs to your large monsters. Using Galaxy Hundred, banish Zeus first and Arise-Heart second.
