Galaxy Hundred rips a card from the opponent's extra deck when Galaxy-Eyes Photon Dragon is summoned. This can be triggered on your turn and your opponent's turn for up to two banishes. A combo's banish count is shown on the right side.

Common ways to trigger Galaxy Hundred on your opponent's turn include the effect of Starliege Photon Blast Dragon, and the effect of Galaxy-Eyes Afterglow Dragon when detached as material.

In the TCG, Galaxy-Eyes Cipher X Dragon applies targeting protection that lingers through the next turn, even if it leaves the field. Combos with cipher protection are marked with +EX on the right side.

Notable cards remaining in your hand, if any, are shown beneath Solflare Dragon. Xyz materials with floating effects are shown beneath their respective monsters.

NS = Normal Summon, SS = Special Summon, GEPD = Galaxy-Eyes Photon Dragon, GPD = Galaxy Photon Dragon
