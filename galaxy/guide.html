Beginner's Guide

What is Galaxy?

Galaxy is a combo deck focused around Rank 8 xyzs and Light monsters with 2000 or higher attack.

The archetype is split into two related groups - Galaxy and Photon - bridged by its namesake monster, Galaxy-Eyes Photon Dragon. There is an emphasis on summoning this monster and using it as material for Dragon xyzs with enormous attack values. Galaxy monsters can end games in a single turn or even a single attack, as well as mount a solid defense going first.

Backing up Galaxy's level 8 monsters are an assortment of low level monsters, spell cards, and trap cards. While Rank 8s are the deck's payoff, the supports are its foundation.

This guide will introduce you to the general goals of the deck. To learn about specific cards in detail, check out the card ratings page.
